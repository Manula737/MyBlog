Lamborghini (officially Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A.) is an iconic Italian manufacturer of luxury sports cars and SUVs, now offering a fully electrified, high-performance vehicle range. The current lineup includes the Revuelto, Temerario, and the Urus SE. Current Lamborghini Models Lamborghini's current production lineup is fully electrified (hybrid and plug-in hybrid models) and is part of their "Direzione Cor Tauri" sustainability plan. Lamborghini Revuelto Overview: The first High Performance Electrified Vehicle (HPEV) and the successor to the Aventador. It is Lamborghini's most powerful production car to date. Performance: It is powered by a new 6.5L naturally aspirated V12 engine combined with three electric motors to produce a total output of over 1,000 horsepower. It can go from 0-62 mph in just 2.4 seconds and has a top speed of over 217 mph. Price: New models start from around £457,095 or an estimated $608,358. Lamborghini Temerario Overview: The successor to the Huracán, the Temerario is a V8-powered plug-in hybrid super sports car unveiled in 2024. Performance: It features a new V8 twin-turbo hybrid engine that delivers 907 HP (920 CV) paired with three electric motors. It boasts enhanced aerodynamics and a new 8-speed dual-clutch transmission. Price: Expected to start at just under $300,000 (around £264,625). Lamborghini Urus SE Overview: The first plug-in hybrid version of the brand's Super SUV, setting a new benchmark in the luxury SUV segment. Performance: It integrates a twin-turbo V8 engine with a 192-hp electric motor for a combined output of 789 horsepower (800 CV), allowing for improved efficiency and enhanced driving dynamics. Price: Starts at around $262,631 for the Hybrid model, while the Urus S and Performante trims are also available.
