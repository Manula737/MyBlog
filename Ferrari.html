Ferrari is an iconic Italian luxury sports car manufacturer known for its high-performance vehicles, cutting-edge technology, and rich racing heritage. Founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1939, the brand has become synonymous with speed, elegance, and exclusivity. Ferrari's lineup includes a range of powerful sports cars and supercars that combine advanced engineering with exquisite design. The company has a storied history in motorsports, particularly in Formula 1 racing, where it has achieved significant success. Ferrari continues to innovate with hybrid and electric technologies while maintaining its commitment to delivering thrilling driving experiences to enthusiasts worldwide. Ferrari S.p.A. is an iconic Italian luxury sports car manufacturer renowned for its racing heritage, high-performance vehicles, and brand exclusivity. Motorsports (Scuderia Ferrari) Ferrari has a deep history in motor racing and is the only team to have competed in every Formula One season since the championship began in 1950. The racing division, Scuderia Ferrari HP, holds records for the most Constructor's Championships (16) and Driver's Championships (15). For the 2025 F1 season, their drivers are Charles Leclerc and seven-time World Champion Lewis Hamilton, driving the SF-25 car. The team faced some technical challenges in the 2025 season, finishing in fourth place in the constructors' standings, leading to an early focus on the 2026 car development.
